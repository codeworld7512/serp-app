Bonita Payments is a merchant account provider and a reseller of Clover POS products. It is an all-in-one payment provider which can process payments, provide physical equipment, and all the tools a business needs to do business online.

The company was founded in 2016 and is currently headquartered in New Orleans, Louisiana. Elliot Forman is listed as the president of the company.

Bonita Payments Overview

Bonita payments came into existence in 2016. The company prides itself on its reliability, transparency, and affordability.

Bonita Payments is a full-fledged merchant account provider that can process all cashless payments. They also offer physical equipment in the form of Clover products.

Bonita Payments claims to offer customized solutions for a wide range of business types. However, they seem to be most passionate about serving small businesses. They specifically cite their expertise in businesses in the following industries: retail, restaurant, gas, mobile and online.

Their prices and contract terms will vary depending on the needs of the business. Overall, their pricing is very good and although little is known about their contract terms, there are no customer complaints about the contract.

Bonita Payments Products & Services

Hardware and Software

Bonita Payments is a reseller of Clover POS products. This includes full POS systems, mini POS systems, credit card readers, and handheld POS systems.

Some of the features that come with the POS systems include a weekly email of data insights, detailed reporting, as well as enhanced speed and security.

Customers will also have access to the Clover Dashboard and the fully customizable Clover Go App.

Some other features include inventory management, enhanced fraud protection, and rapid deposit.

Some of the features they offer include:

Authorize.Net which provides a secure infrastructure for fast payments.

PayeezySM eCommerce allows multiple payment options and enhanced security.

eProcessing Network, which offers secure, real-time processing of cards, checks, and gift cards whether in-person, online or via a mobile app.
